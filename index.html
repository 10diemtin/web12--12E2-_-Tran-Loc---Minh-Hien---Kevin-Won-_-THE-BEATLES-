THE BEATLES

All you need is love.

The Beatles were an iconic British rock band formed in Liverpool in 1960, consisting of John Lennon, Paul McCartney, George Harrison, and Ringo Starr. Widely regarded as one of the most influential bands in the history of music, The Beatles revolutionized popular culture with their innovative sound, creative songwriting, and charismatic personalities. From their early hits like "Love Me Do" and "She Loves You" to their groundbreaking albums such as "Sgt. Pepper’s Lonely Hearts Club Band" and "Abbey Road", The Beatles redefined what modern music could be. Their legacy continues to inspire generations of musicians and fans around the world, making them not just a band, but a timeless cultural phenomenon.
